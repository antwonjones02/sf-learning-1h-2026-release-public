View Recommended Assignments and Learning Opportunities for Development Goals

Feature #2 of 32
End UserAdmin

What Is This Feature?

Users can now view assignments and learning opportunities that match the skills and attributes selected for their development goals, directly on their goal details page in an Opportunities to Grow section. Users can bookmark recommended items, mark them as not interested, link assignments to goals (shown in a Linked Assignments section, printable), and add learning opportunities through a Linked Learning section. A Go to Opportunity Marketplace link is also provided for broader exploration.

What Is Changing?

A new Opportunities to Grow section appears on users' own development goal details pages, showing matching assignments and learning opportunities. Users can now link learning and assignments directly to development goals.

Why Should We Care?

Connecting recommended learning and assignments to specific development goals helps employees pursue skill-focused career development within a structured and measurable framework.

Risk of Inaction

Users will not see skill-aligned learning recommendations on their development goals page, missing an opportunity to connect learning activity to career development objectives.
