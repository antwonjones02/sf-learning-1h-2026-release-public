New Advanced Settings to Manage Zoom Virtual Learning Sessions

Feature #5 of 32
Admin

What Is This Feature?

Two new advanced settings have been introduced on the Advanced VLS Settings page (Classes > Agenda > Edit Time Slot) for Zoom servers: Allow Multiple Devices and Approval Type. These settings provide additional control over how Zoom virtual learning sessions are configured at the class time slot level, extending the options available to admins managing virtual instructor-led training via Zoom.

What Is Changing?

Two new settings — Allow Multiple Devices and Approval Type — are added to the Advanced VLS Settings page for Zoom. Previously these options were not configurable from within SAP SuccessFactors Learning.

Why Should We Care?

Organizations using Zoom for virtual instructor-led training gain finer control over session access and device policies, enabling better alignment with security and attendance requirements.

Risk of Inaction

Without configuring the new Zoom settings, the default Zoom behavior applies. Organizations with specific device or approval policies may need to configure these to meet their requirements.
