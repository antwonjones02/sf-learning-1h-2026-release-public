Optional Curricula in the New Learning Experience for iOS and Android Mobile Apps

Feature #19 of 32
End User

What Is This Feature?

Learners can now access optional learning activities from their assigned curricula and sub-curricula directly from the new Learning home screen on the SAP SuccessFactors Mobile iOS and Android apps. Admin-assigned optional activities appear in the Important Learning section; self-assigned optional activities appear in the Invest in Myself section. All items display a Curriculum label on the card.

What Is Changing?

Optional curriculum items are now surfaced on the mobile Learning home screen. Previously they were not visible or accessible via the new Learning experience on mobile, limiting functional parity with the web.

Why Should We Care?

Learners on mobile devices can now see and access the full range of their curriculum content, including optional items, ensuring consistent development opportunities across web and mobile.

Risk of Inaction

No action needed — automatically enabled. Requires the new Learning experience to be enabled and optional curricula to be configured in Learning Administration.

Recommendation No action required

Impact Minor
