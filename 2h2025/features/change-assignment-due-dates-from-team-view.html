Change Assignment Due Dates From Team View

Feature #1 of 32
Manager

What Is This Feature?

Managers can now change assignment due dates for their direct reports directly from the Team View page, either individually or in bulk. The Change Due Dates dialog lists all assigned items for a subordinate so managers can update dates without leaving Team View. This is part of a broader redesigned Team View experience delivered in response to Customer Influence Idea 352489.

What Is Changing?

A new Change Due Dates dialog is added to Team View, enabling individual and bulk due date changes. Previously this functionality was not available from Team View at all.

Why Should We Care?

Managers gain a faster, self-service way to adjust learning deadlines without relying on admins, reducing friction in day-to-day learning management.
