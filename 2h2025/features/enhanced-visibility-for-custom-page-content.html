Enhanced Visibility for Custom Page Content

Feature #11 of 32
Admin

What Is This Feature?

Admins can now independently control whether banners and custom cards are shown or hidden on individual custom pages, without affecting their visibility on the new Learning home page. The Enabled switch in System Administration > Application Administration > Custom Pages manages this per-page visibility. By default, visibility reflects the existing settings from the Banners and Custom Cards pages. Based on Customer Influence Idea 333734.

What Is Changing?

A new Enabled switch allows per-page visibility control for banners and custom cards on custom pages. Previously, content visible on the Learning home page was always displayed on custom pages as well, with no independent control.

Why Should We Care?

Organizations can now target different content to different audiences on custom pages without impacting the main Learning home page, enabling more precise and relevant learner experiences.
