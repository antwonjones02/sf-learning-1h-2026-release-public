Enhanced Support for Secondary Instructors in Virtual Learning Sessions

Feature #6 of 32
AdminEnd User

What Is This Feature?

Secondary instructors can now launch and manage assigned Virtual Learning Sessions (VLS) directly from the My Classes dashboard on the Learning home page, just like primary instructors, across Microsoft Teams, Zoom, and Cisco Webex. They now receive automatic email notifications with personalized meeting links and vCalendar invites. Attendance calculation now includes session activity from both primary and secondary instructors. Based on Customer Influence Ideas 284474 and 273147.

What Is Changing?

Secondary instructors gain Start Session and Join Session capabilities from their dashboard. Automatic notifications with vCalendar invites are sent when they are assigned. Attendance now tracks both primary and secondary instructor activity.

Why Should We Care?

Secondary instructors can independently manage and participate in virtual sessions without relying on the primary instructor's account, improving operational flexibility for multi-instructor training.

Risk of Inaction

Without enabling this feature, secondary instructors continue to require the primary instructor's account to launch VLS, limiting their ability to independently manage sessions.
