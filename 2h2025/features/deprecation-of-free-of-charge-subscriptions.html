Deprecation of Free-of-Charge Subscriptions

Feature #3 of 32
Admin

What Is This Feature?

The ability to create a free-of-charge subscription to override all pricing within a library is deprecated and will be deleted on May 14, 2027. This feature is not actively used by customers in production and is not supported by the enhanced search feature, which is a core component of the new Learning home page. SAP changed the deletion date multiple times; the current target is May 14, 2027.

What Is Changing?

Free-of-charge subscription creation reached End of Maintenance on May 17, 2024 and will be fully deleted on May 14, 2027. The feature will be removed from the application at the Deleted milestone.

Why Should We Care?

Organizations using free-of-charge subscriptions for pricing overrides need to identify and migrate to supported pricing and access control mechanisms before the May 2027 deletion date.

Risk of Inaction

Customers still relying on free-of-charge subscriptions will lose this functionality on May 14, 2027. SAP does not fix bugs or deliver patches for this feature following the End of Maintenance date.

Recommendation Evaluate with SAP rep
