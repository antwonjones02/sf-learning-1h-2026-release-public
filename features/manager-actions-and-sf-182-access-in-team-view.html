Manager Actions and SF-182 Access in Team View

Feature #4 of 34
AdminManager

What Is This Feature?

Managers can now register team members for learning, perform withdrawal and removal actions, and access SF-182 requests, reports, and curricula status directly from Team View.

What Is Changing?

Team View becomes a centralized hub for managers to take action on their team's learning, no more navigating to separate pages.

Why Should We Care?

Streamlines the manager experience and increases manager engagement in team development. Reduces administrative friction.

Risk of Inaction

No action needed, automatically enabled. Managers will see new capabilities immediately.

Recommendation Communicate to managers
