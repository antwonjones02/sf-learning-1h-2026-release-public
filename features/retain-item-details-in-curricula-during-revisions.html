Retain Item Details in Curricula During Revisions

Feature #25 of 34
End UserAdmin

What Is This Feature?

Initial and retraining assignment data for items in a curriculum is now preserved during item revisions through the Item Connector.

What Is Changing?

When an item within a curriculum is revised, the assignment and retraining data is no longer wiped out.

Why Should We Care?

Prevents loss of compliance-critical data during routine content updates. Protects training history integrity.

Risk of Inaction

Item revisions may still clear assignment data within curricula, creating compliance tracking gaps.

Recommendation Enable
Type Changed
Impact Minor
