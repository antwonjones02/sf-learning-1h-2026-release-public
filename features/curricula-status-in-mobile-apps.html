Curricula Status in Mobile Apps

Feature #3 of 34
End UserAdmin

What Is This Feature?

Learners can now view their curricula assignments and track compliance status directly in the SAP SuccessFactors Mobile iOS and Android apps.

What Is Changing?

Curricula tracking (previously only available on desktop) is now fully accessible on mobile devices.

Why Should We Care?

Frontline and field workers who primarily use mobile can now monitor their compliance status on the go, reducing overdue assignments.

Risk of Inaction

No action needed, automatically enabled for mobile users.

Recommendation No action required
