Assisted Skills Association With OCN Items, GA

Feature #26 of 34
Admin

What Is This Feature?

The AI-assisted skills association feature for Open Content Network items, previously in early adoption, is now generally available.

What Is Changing?

AI automatically suggests relevant skills to associate with OCN content items. The feature moves from beta to full production readiness.

Why Should We Care?

Scales skills tagging across large third-party content libraries. Enables skills-based learning recommendations.

Risk of Inaction

Skills must be manually tagged to OCN items, impractical at scale. Skills-based recommendations remain incomplete.
