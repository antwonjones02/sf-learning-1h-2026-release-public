Person-Centric Experience for Multiple Employments

Feature #49 of 34
End User

What Is This Feature?

Administrators can now manage assignments, completions, history, and employment data at the person level for learners with multiple employments in Employee Central.

What Is Changing?

Learning management shifts from an employment-centric to a person-centric model for users with multiple jobs.

Why Should We Care?

Eliminates duplicate assignments and confusion for employees who hold multiple roles. Gives a single view of learning history.

Risk of Inaction

Employees with multiple roles continue to see fragmented learning records. Higher admin burden to reconcile.

Recommendation Contact Product Support

Impact Major
