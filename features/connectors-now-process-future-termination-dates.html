Connectors Now Process Future Termination Dates

Feature #10 of 54
Admin

What Is This Feature?

Learning connectors now process user records that have future termination dates, ensuring data remains synchronized before the termination takes effect.

What Is Changing?

Previously, connectors would skip users with future termination dates. Now these records are processed normally until the termination date arrives.

Why Should We Care?

Prevents data gaps for employees in their notice period. Ensures learning assignments and completions are properly tracked through end of employment.

Risk of Inaction

No action needed, automatically applied.

Recommendation No action required
Type Changed
Impact Minor
