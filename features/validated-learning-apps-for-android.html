Validated Learning Apps for Android

Feature #7 of 34
End User

What Is This Feature?

The SAP Validated Learning and SAP Validated Learning Preview apps are now available for Android mobile devices.

What Is Changing?

Android users in regulated industries now have access to the validated (GxP-compliant) Learning apps, previously only available on iOS.

Why Should We Care?

Extends compliance-grade mobile learning to a much larger user base. Critical for organizations in life sciences, manufacturing, and other regulated sectors.

Risk of Inaction

No action needed, automatically available in app stores.

Recommendation Enable for regulated teams
