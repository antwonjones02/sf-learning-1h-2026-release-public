Enhancements to Analytic Cards on Home Page

Feature #29 of 34
End UserAdmin

What Is This Feature?

New enhancements to analytic cards include a Total Learning Hours card and new administration options for managing cards on the latest home page.

What Is Changing?

A new Total Learning Hours card provides at-a-glance insight. Admins get more control over which analytic cards appear.

Why Should We Care?

Gives leaders quick visibility into learning engagement metrics without running reports.

Risk of Inaction

Leadership misses a quick engagement metric. Admins have less control over the home page experience.
